Sandwiches!

YUM

Food functions as the epicenter of my home. When I think back to my early childhood, I remember condensed milk toast. The warm, sticky-soft of the condensed milk-soaked toast is absolutely delicious. When on the table, we would split it and share it. This sense of togetherness and comfort is home.


Although I can easily cook my own meals, nothing beats the taste of food made by my family.


The grilled cheese my dad made in Jan 2023 sparked the idea for this work. My mom and I had just bought sourdough, my sister made garlic confit, and my dad had a craving. The final pan-toasted grilled cheese had smooth garlic and white Kraft American cheese. There was structure, texture, and a gorgeous meltiness.


And so, that got me thinking. Sandwiches!

What is a sandwich? Let's find out.


I asked people in my community to define "sandwich" and share their favorite examples.
